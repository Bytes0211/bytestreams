Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how bytestreams ("Company", "we", "us", and "our") handles cookies and similar tracking technologies on https://bytestreams.ai ("Website").

Current status: We do not use cookies on this Website.

Do we use cookies?

No. bytestreams.ai does not set first-party cookies, does not use third-party advertising cookies, and does not run cookie-based analytics.

Do we use similar tracking technologies?

No. We do not use tracking pixels, web beacons, or browser fingerprinting technologies on this Website for advertising or behavioral tracking.

How does this align with our services?

ByteStreams and related DialTone flows are primarily SMS-driven rather than browser-session-driven. Customer interactions are handled through phone and SMS channels, not cookie-based web user profiling.

What information do we collect on the Website?

The Website may collect information you actively submit (for example, through contact forms or direct email communication). That collection is not cookie-based.

SMS and third-party providers

SMS delivery for our product workflows may involve telecommunications providers (for example, Twilio) in operational systems. Those SMS operations are separate from cookie use on this Website and do not depend on cookies in your browser.

Do I need to change browser cookie settings?

Since we do not use cookies on this Website, no cookie preference tool is required for bytestreams.ai at this time. You can still manage browser cookie settings globally if you prefer.

How often will this policy be updated?

We may update this Cookie Policy if our practices change. If we begin using cookies or similar tracking technologies, we will update this page and revise the date shown at the top.

Where can I get more information?

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please email us at privacy@bytestreams.com.

About Bytestreams

Bytestreams is an engineering studio that builds AI-native products for industries that can't afford to get it wrong.

We've shipped production systems across healthcare, pharmaceutical research, medical devices, and large-scale data platforms for over two decades. Our own products — DialTone, StratifyAI, AIphaWatch — run on the same stack we bring to client engagements: AWS, Terraform, real-time streaming, and production-grade AI integration.

We don't chase volume. We take on work where the fit is obvious, ship something maintainable, and let the code speak for itself.

Twenty years in the code, not the conference circuit

Bytestreams is led by engineers who have been shipping production systems for two decades — across healthcare, pharma, medical devices, and large-scale data platforms. You're not paying for someone's first AWS project. You're paying for pattern recognition earned over a career.

AI/LLM work that actually runs in production

RAG pipelines serving Bedrock-powered chatbots. LangGraph agentic workflows. Multi-provider LLM orchestration. Vector retrieval at scale. MCP-based automation. This isn't "we also do AI" — it's what we ship for ourselves (DialTone, StratifyAI) and for clients every week.

Healthcare and life sciences fluency

Sterling Pharma. Stryker Medical. Sonafi clinical research. Wright Medical. Accredo Health. Bytestreams has shipped production systems inside healthcare and life sciences for years. HIPAA workflows, regulated data handling, and domain vocabulary aren't a learning curve — they're background knowledge.

End-to-end stack, one accountability chain

Data engineering (Kafka, Flink, Spark, Databricks, Delta Lake). Cloud infrastructure (AWS, Terraform, multi-petabyte production data lakes). Backend (Python, FastAPI). Frontend (React, Svelte). ML/LLM integration (PyTorch, Bedrock, LangChain). One team, one point of accountability — not three vendors blaming each other.

Built to be handed off

Every engagement ships with Terraform, tests, CI/CD, and documentation. When we finish, your team inherits something maintainable — not tribal knowledge. Our own product (StratifyAI) has 877 tests at 85% coverage; client work meets the same bar.

Proof you can inspect before we ever talk

Published PyPI package. Open-source projects on GitHub. Live deployed products (DialTone, AIphaWatch). Public portfolio with real metrics. Before a single sales call, you can see how we build. The work speaks first.

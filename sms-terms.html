SMS Terms & Consent

Last updated: April 22, 2026

ByteStreams LLC operates DialTone, an AI voice ordering platform. When you place an order through DialTone, we may send a Stripe payment link, payment confirmation, and order status updates by SMS. This page explains consent, message types, opt-out behavior, and support.

1. About ByteStreams and DialTone

ByteStreams LLC is a Tennessee-registered company. Restaurants using DialTone are the sender of record to their customers. ByteStreams provides the communication infrastructure and consent-capture tooling.

2. Consent and Opt-In

You will only receive SMS messages when consent is provided, including voice consent during calls or checkbox consent during online ordering. No promotional SMS is sent without separate, explicit opt-in.

Voice consent script includes payment-link disclosure, message frequency notice, potential carrier charges, and opt-out instructions.

3. Message Types

  • Payment link (typically 1 per order)
  • Payment confirmation (typically 1 per order)
  • Order-ready or status updates (typically 1-2 per order)
  • Reservation reminder where supported
  • Promotional messages only for separately opted-in users
  • HELP response messages on request

4. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan and carrier.

5. How to Opt Out

Reply with any supported keyword to stop messages: STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT. You will receive a final confirmation after opt-out.

6. How to Get Help

Reply HELP or INFO to receive support details. You can also contact ByteStreams directly at hello@bytestreams.ai or 629-282-9555.

7. Supported Carriers

DialTone traffic uses registered 10DLC and is supported by major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

8. Privacy and Data Sharing

SMS opt-in data is not sold and is not shared with third parties for their marketing purposes. Data is shared only with necessary delivery providers and the restaurant receiving your order.

9. Eligibility

SMS messaging is intended for recipients 18+ or otherwise legally able to consent under applicable law.

10. Recording and Retention

Voice opt-ins are recorded and retained as proof of consent. Website opt-ins are logged with timestamp, IP address, and consent language.

11. Restaurant Responsibilities

Restaurants using DialTone are responsible for messaging only users with valid consent. ByteStreams provides platform infrastructure and compliance tooling.

12. Updates to These Terms

These terms may be updated to reflect legal, carrier, or platform changes. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
